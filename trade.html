How Trading Works on AEMO

  1. Select a verified trader from the marketplace.
  2. Enter the amount you want to exchange.
  3. Funds are held securely in AEMO escrow.
  4. The exchanger completes the currency transfer.
  5. You confirm receipt before funds are released.
  6. Both users rate the transaction.
